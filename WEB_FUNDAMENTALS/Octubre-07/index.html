Michael Schumacher

Hürth-Hermülheim,Germany

Michael Schumacher is a retired German racing driver who raced in Formula One for Jordan, Benetton, Ferrari and Mercedes. He is a seven-time World Champion and is widely regarded as one of the greatest Formula One drivers of all time.

Schumacher was born in Hürth, North Rhine-Westphalia, to Rolf Schumacher, a bricklayer, and his wife Elisabeth. When Schumacher was four, his father modified his pedal kart by adding a small motorcycle engine. When Schumacher crashed it into a lamp post in Kerpen, his parents took him to the karting track at Kerpen-Horrem, where he became the youngest member of the karting club.
